Feel

Pronunciation: /fiÀêl/

Definition: To perceive or experience an emotion or sensation through physical or mental awareness.

Example Sentence: I feel happy when I spend time with my friends.

Contextual Information

The verb "feel" can refer to both physical sensations (like touching something) and emotional states (like feeling sad or excited). It's a versatile word used in various contexts, such as expressing emotions, physical sensations, or even intuitions about situations.
